## What I'm Building

### [PediaHelper.com](https://pediahelper.com)
Pediatric learning and clinical support platform.

| Tool | What it does |
|------|-------------|
| [Ask Nelson](https://pediahelper.com/ask-nelson) | AI-assisted rapid revision based on Nelson concepts |
| [Ask Evidence](https://pediahelper.com/ask-evidence) | PubMed-backed evidence search with AI summaries |
| SyndromeAssist | Dysmorphology and syndrome identification support |
| Calculators | Pediatric dosing and clinical references |

### [PocketMed AI](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.vivek.pocketmed)
Offline-first clinical assistant for Android.
On-device AI. Drug dosing. Differentials. Lab interpretation. No internet needed.
Built for real hospital workflows in low-resource settings.

### [easy-pg.com](https://easy-pg.com)
AI-assisted exam prep for PG entrance and residency exams.

### [drvivek.com.np](https://drvivek.com.np)
Experimental conversational AI healthcare platform.
